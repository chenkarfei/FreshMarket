# FreshMarket User Manual

Welcome to the FreshMarket application! This guide explains how to use the platform based on your assigned role: **Restaurant**, **Admin**, or **Super Admin**.

---

## 1. Restaurant User Guide
The Restaurant role is designed for customers who need to browse the catalog, place daily orders, and track their order history.

### Browsing and Ordering
*   **Dashboard:** Upon logging in, you will see the main catalog grouped by categories (e.g., Vegetables, Seafood, Poultry).
*   **Adding to Cart:** Click the **"Add to Cart"** (加入购物车) button under any item to add it to your current order.
*   **Language:** You can toggle between English (EN) and Chinese (ZH) using the language button in the top right corner.

### Managing Your Cart
*   **View Cart:** Click the shopping cart icon to view your selected items.
*   **Adjust Quantities:** Use the **+** and **-** buttons to adjust the quantity of each item.
*   **Submit Order:** Once you are satisfied with your list, click **"Submit Order"** to send it to the admin team.

### Order History
*   Navigate to the **Orders** tab to view your past and current orders.
*   You can check the status of your orders (e.g., Pending, Acknowledged, Completed).

---

## 2. Admin User Guide
The Admin role is responsible for managing the daily operations, including processing orders, updating the catalog, and generating purchase reports.

### Dashboard Overview
*   The Admin Dashboard provides a quick glance at **Tonight's Orders**, **Estimated Revenue**, and **Active Restaurants**.

### Managing Orders
*   **Orders Tab:** View all orders submitted by restaurants.
*   **Acknowledge Orders:** Review the items requested and click **"Acknowledge Order"** to confirm receipt and begin processing.

### Managing Categories
*   **Categories Tab:** View all item categories.
*   **Add/Edit:** Click "Add Category" to create a new one, or "Edit" to modify an existing one. You can provide both English and Chinese names for dynamic translation.
*   **Reorder:** Use the drag handle (six dots) to drag and drop categories to change the order in which they appear to restaurants.

### Managing Items
*   **Items Tab:** Manage the actual products available for sale.
*   **Add/Edit Items:** You can set the item's name (EN/ZH), assign it to a category, set the unit (e.g., kg, pack), and define the estimated price range.
*   **Active Status:** Use the "Active/Inactive" toggle to hide items from the restaurant view if they are out of stock, without deleting them.

### Consolidated Purchase Report
*   **Report Tab:** This tab automatically compiles all acknowledged orders for the selected date into a single master shopping list.
*   **Print:** Click the **"Print List"** button to generate a printer-friendly version of the consolidated list for your purchasing team.

---

## 3. Super Admin User Guide
The Super Admin has all the capabilities of a standard Admin, plus the authority to manage system users and assign roles.

### User Management
*   **Users Tab:** This tab is exclusively available to Super Admins.
*   **View Users:** See a list of all registered accounts on the platform.
*   **Assign Roles:** Change a user's role by selecting from the dropdown menu (e.g., making a new user a "Restaurant", "Admin", or "Driver").
*   **Access Control:** Ensure only authorized personnel have Admin or Super Admin access to protect sensitive business data.
